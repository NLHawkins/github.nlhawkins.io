Fido Allows a user to chose their preferred beer in three ways: a list of favorites, a list of nationally available beers or a search for a different beer.





After a user chooses a beer they know they would enjoy, Fido compares the attributes of this beer to those available at the drinker's location. Fido then returns the best match for the user's taste.





Matches are shown sided my side allowing users to compare the similar attributes of their beer (highlighted in yellow). A user also has options to view detailed descriptions and save beers as favorties and matches to reference later.

Description

As microbreweries grow in popularity the average consumer finds them-self faced with a greater number of obscure beer choices at their local pub. Fido allows a user to choose a beer he or she knows they would enjoy and see matches based on specific attributes of that beer.
